"Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani"(2013) is a Bollywood coming-of-age film directed by Ayan Mukerji, starring Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapur, and Kalki Koechlin. The story revolves around four friends—Kabir (Ranbir), Naina (Deepika), Aditi (Kalki), and Avi (Aditya)—as they navigate their youth, relationships, and personal growth. The film begins with a trekking trip during college and spans several years, showcasing their evolving dreams, love, and friendship. Kabir's carefree attitude gradually transforms as he learns the importance of love and relationships. With a memorable soundtrack and vibrant visuals, the film emphasizes themes of friendship, self-discovery, and love, becoming a massive commercial success and a beloved film for its relatable portrayal of life's journey.
